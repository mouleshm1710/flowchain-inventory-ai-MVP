# FlowChain Inventory Risk Intelligence AI

## Problem Statement
Many businesses face challenges in managing inventory efficiently due to fluctuating demand patterns, limited visibility into inventory levels, and delayed decision-making. These challenges often result in stockouts, leading to missed sales opportunities, or excess inventory, increasing holding costs and wastage. 
Additionally, the lack of simple analytical tools to proactively identify such risks makes it difficult for supply chain teams to respond effectively in a timely manner.

## Objective
The objective of this project is to build an AI-powered inventory risk intelligence system that analyzes historical demand and inventory data to identify potential risks such as stockouts and overstock situations. The system aims to provide simple, data-driven insights and basic recommendations to support decision-making for supply chain stakeholders. 
The focus for the MVP phase is to develop a lightweight, interactive application that enables users to upload data, visualize trends, and detect inventory risks using analytical logic.

## Expected Outcome
The expected outcome of the MVP is a functional prototype that allows users to upload inventory datasets and receive insights related to demand trends and inventory risks. The system will highlight potential stockout and overstock scenarios and provide basic recommendations to guide decision-making. This prototype will demonstrate the feasibility of applying analytics and AI techniques to real-world supply chain problems and serve as a foundation for further enhancements such as forecasting models and intelligent recommendations.
